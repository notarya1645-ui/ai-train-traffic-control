Reports

AI is transforming railway traffic management by using real-time data and machine learning to control train movement. How It Works: Collects data from sensors, GPS, and signals. AI analyzes delays, risks, and traffic flow. Suggests route changes, speed control, or rescheduling. Benefits: Safer operations (collision prevention). Less delays, better schedules. Saves energy and reduces costs. Predicts maintenance needs. Challenges: High costs, old infrastructure, and cybersecurity risks.
